About

I am creative and driven UX/UI designer, with the unlimited ability for sarcasm and illustrations. Through my illustration work, I am trying to bring abstract ideas into the real world and communicate them to the observers.

During my psychology degree, I developed a deeper understanding of and gained profound insights into human behavior. Throughout the course, I applied this knowledge to the different facets of B2C interactions.

As I was always interested in the field of technology, I decided to combine all 3 areas of knowledge into my future career path and joined the UX design Bootcamp. There, I obtained all the necessary tools to become a user advocate and problem-solver for companies.
